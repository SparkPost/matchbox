### Accessibility

Use this contrast checker to find accessible text and background pairings. See <ExternalLink to='https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#contrast-minimum'>1.4.3 Minimum (AA) Requirements</ExternalLink> and <ExternalLink to='https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#contrast-enhanced'>1.4.6 Enhanced (AAA) Requirements</ExternalLink> for details.

- Contrast ratio must be at least **4.5** for normal text
- Contrast ratio must be at least **3.0** for large text _(18px, or <Token name='font-size-300' />)_
